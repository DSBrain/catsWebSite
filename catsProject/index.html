Cat Information

About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, grace, and independent nature.

Types of Cats

  • Persian
  • Siamese
  • Maine Coon
  • Bengal
  • Sphynx

Fun Facts

Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four toes on their back paws. This is normal for cats, but some cats have extra toes, a condition called polydactylism.
